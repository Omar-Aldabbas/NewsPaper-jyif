Give people courage

The crowd seemed to grow

The sunset faded before anything further happened. The crowd towards Woking seemed to grow, and a faint murmur rose. The people near Chobham dispersed. There was little movement from the pit.

It was this stillness that gave people courage. As dusk came on, a slow movement toward the sand pits began, gaining force as the cylinder lay silent. Figures advanced in a thin crescent to enclose the pit. I, too, began to move closer.

Soon, cabmen and others boldly entered the sand pits, wheels clattered, and a lad trundled a barrow of apples. Advancing from Horsell, a knot of men approached, one waving a white flag.
